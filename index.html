Terms & Conditions

1. Driver Eligibility

The renter must be at least 21 years old and possess a valid driving license.

2. Vehicle Usage

Vehicles must be used only for lawful purposes. Racing, off-road driving, and illegal activities are strictly prohibited.

3. Rental Period

The vehicle must be returned on or before the agreed return date and time. Late returns may incur additional charges.

4. Security Deposit

A refundable security deposit may be required before vehicle delivery.

5. Fuel Policy

Vehicles must be returned with the same fuel level as provided at pickup.

6. Damage & Accidents

Any accident, damage, or mechanical issue must be reported immediately. Repair costs resulting from negligence will be charged to the renter.

7. Cancellation Policy

Cancellations made 24 hours before pickup may be eligible for a refund. Late cancellations may incur charges.

8. Insurance

Basic insurance may be included. Additional coverage options may be available.

9. Prohibited Activities

Subletting, transporting illegal goods, or allowing unauthorized drivers to operate the vehicle is prohibited.

10. Agreement

By booking a vehicle, you agree to all terms and conditions stated above.
